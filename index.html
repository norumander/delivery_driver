Welcome to my 2D MVP Practice Projects

All credits go to the GameDev.tv 2D course!

  • Snowboarder

    Press up to accelerate and left or right to tilt. Can you make it to the finish?

  • Metroidvania

    Arrow keys to move right and left, up to climb, click to fire and spacebar to jump. 3 levels of fun! Can you find the shortcut on the last one?

  • Delivery Driver

    Up arrow to move, left and right to rotate. Pick up packages and deliver them without losing any speed!

  • Laser Defender

    Space bar to toggle shooting, arrow keys to move arround. Fire away and blast those pesky aliens!
